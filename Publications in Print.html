Hello I'm

Joan Ponce

I'm Presidential Postdoctoral Scholar at the School of Mathematics and Statistical Sciences, I specialize in interdisciplinary research at the intersection of mathematics and biology. My work involves developing and analyzing mathematical models to better understand and predict disease dynamics. My research interests encompass dynamical systems, bifurcation analysis, parameter estimation, and geospatial modeling, key elements in shaping public health policies
